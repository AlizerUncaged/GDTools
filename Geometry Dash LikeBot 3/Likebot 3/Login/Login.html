Welcome to GDL-3!

GD Likebot 3 is a like and dislike exchange site which directly uses exposed Geometry Dash APIs.

Can I trust this site?
Yep, your accounts are stored deep into GDL-3 database, no one else has access. Use an alt account for logging in and see for yourself.

How does this work?
Your logged-in account will be used to likebot other people's levels, comments, and posts, while you will have your levels, comments, and posts liked by other people's logged-in accounts as well!

Will my account get banned?
Accounts that are logged in are often banned, so please use an alternate account, an account that isn't your main.

What happens when my account gets banned?
You need to create a new account again to use Likebot 3.

Can I use more accounts than just 1?
Yes! Additional accounts equals increased likes/dislikes.

My level/comment/post got dislikebotted, what should I do?
You are welcome to likebot it back; I take no responsibility for any misuse of this program. The players who committed such acts have their own free will; GDL-3 is just a tool that may be utilized for good or evil. Please don't beg for help with your dislikebotted levels in my DMs.
